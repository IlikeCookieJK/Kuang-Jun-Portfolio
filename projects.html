These are projects that I have worked on. All of these projects were made in Visual Studio Code. They are all organized from latest to oldest. In the carousel, it contains the favorite projects that I have worked on.

Battleship Unknown

This was a game that I created with my partner, Taha. We knew that we needed to make a game that was compatible on desktop and mobile. In addition, the game needed the ability to allow people to play with a friend or computer opponent. Hence why we chose to make a Battleship game with some twists. The game mechanic is simple which made it easier for us to make a computer opponent. After we decided what game we wanted to make, we started by creating a layout of our game in Figma. We then moved on to making a turn flowchart and a program flowchart.

Western Dino House

Western Dino House was built to look like an actual fast food restaurant website. It was made with my partner, Matthew. We spent a week making a layout of our website. Our next step was writing the HTML for each pages. The last step for our project was working on the CSS, but we struggled with positioning the contents in our website. Due to the fact that we lacked the skills, we spent a majority of our time on CSS. Additionaly, we had some JavaScript file that we had to integrate into our website. The file was given to us by our instructor and the purpose was to make the shopping portion of our website functional.
