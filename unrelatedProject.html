Other Projects

These are projects that I have worked on for other classes or for fun.

Paper Straw Rocket

This was a rocket I had to make for engineering class. I had to start with creating a model of my rocket in Fusion 360. Then I got to decide how my fins and nose of my rocket would look like. After I finished creating a model of my rocket, I had to start building it. The body of the rocket was a rolled up piece of paper. Some of the other part of the rocket like the nose and bulkhead were 3D printed. While the fins were laser cutted. The next part was assembling the parts. The last part was launching the rocket and collect data. Data that were collected was the velocity, distance traveled, and a estimated of how high it went.

Glaze Ceramic Bowl

This was my first ceramic project where I pratice how to pinch. I started with manipulating the clay into a sphere. Then I had to press my thumb into clay without going through the sphere. After my thumb left half an inch of clay from the bottom, I started pressing the clay outward in circular while rotating the clay. As I did this more the clay start resembling a bowl. When the bowl was formed, I added a foot to it, incised it for decoration, and it then got fired. After it got fired, it then got glazed and fired again.

Ceramic Whistle

In my ceramic class, we learned how to pinch and score clay. With these combined together, I was able create a clay whistle. I started with pinching the clay into two bowls with similar diameter. Then I scored the clay and blended the two bowl together. The next step was making a hole and attach a cylinder with a hole to the whistle. The last step was decorating the clay after it was fired.
